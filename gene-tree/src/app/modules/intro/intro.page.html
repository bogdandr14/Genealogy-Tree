Welcome to Gene-Tree

The Gene-Tree app is a practical app for managing your personal genealogical tree by keeping trace of your relatives and also finding new ones.

Next

What is Gene-Tree?

Gene-Tree is an app that allows you, the user, to create your own genealogical tree and also extend it with how many relatives you have.

Next

Your information is safe

Gene-Tree does not, and will never use or show the information about your genalogy tree to anyone, beside your relatives that you added.

Next

More and more relatives!

Gene-Tree also offers the possibility of connecting with other relatives that use the app. This way you will have access to their in genealogy tree where you can find even more relatives that you never knew!
